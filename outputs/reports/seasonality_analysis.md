# Seasonality Analysis

## Chapter-Ready Summary

The monthly seasonality analysis shows that Grape has the strongest seasonal pattern, with a seasonality strength of 0.6870, peaking in month 9 and bottoming in month 7. By contrast, Pear shows the weakest seasonal structure, with a seasonality strength of 0.2217. This result can be used to support the thesis discussion on seasonal fluctuations and to justify why seasonal candidates such as SARIMA are only retained for selected fruit series.
